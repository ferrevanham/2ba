Hey, I'm Ferre van Ham

As a designer, the thing that motivates me the most is the act of creating an experience. My purpose is not to work on client work that does not support my vision. I only help clients which I support and want to add value to. Designing is not something I do just to get a living, I do it because it is fun and it excites me. I want to inspire people not only in a visual way but in a lifestyle and vision of joy and happiness. I want to support those which have not yet found themselves in life and are still struggling, I hear you! That's wy I do not only design but I also want to share valuable life lessons.

Just work!

I sometimes do not like finding reason behind my creative work. Sometimes you just have to start playing with shapes and creative tools to create something purely visual. It can be a very relaxing way of getting towards an end result.

Lately I got a small creative block because I wanted to make everything I make with a clear thought behind it. I then got stuck in my head not knowing exactly what I wanted to make. Because of that I couldn't get started on creative work.

You have to get rid of the idea of perfection and just get going.
